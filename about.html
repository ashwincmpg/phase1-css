Our Story

Started with Love, Built on Dough

Domino's Pizza was founded in 1960 when Tom Monaghan and his brother, James Monaghan, purchased a small pizza store called DomiNick's in Ypsilanti, Michigan. In 1965, Tom became the sole owner and renamed the business Domino's Pizza, laying the foundation for what would become one of the world's largest pizza brands.

Through a commitment to quality, fast delivery, and continuous innovation, Domino's expanded rapidly across the United States and around the world. The company pioneered pizza delivery services and embraced technology, making it easier than ever for customers to order their favorite pizzas.

Today, Domino's operates thousands of stores in more than 90 countries and territories, serving millions of customers every week. While the company has grown globally, it remains focused on delivering delicious pizzas, excellent service, and a convenient ordering experience.
